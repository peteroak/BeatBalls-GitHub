Bubble Football

The game that will make you cry from laughing!

The Game

Bubble Football is the sport that takes a simple football game to the next level. It is played inside inflatable bumpers, which allows the players, children or adults, to bump into each other in a safe and fun way.

Fields

It is played in 5x5 football fields, which can be of synthetic/natural grass or even in five-a-side soccer courts.

Players

It is played in teams of 4, without a goalkeeper and with smaller goals. As it is a physically demanding sport, we always recommend teams of at leats 6 players!

Rules

The only fault is to take off your bumper while on the field.

Why Play Bubble Football?

Have you thought of playing a footbal game with no faults nor worries? Then Bubble Football is the answer! Forget about and red cards. Inside the bumpers, your only concern should be to run fast, (try) to kick the ball and bump into your friends!
